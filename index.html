About Me

I am currently a senior at Indiana University Bloomington studying web and app design, game development, data science, and computer science. I have been coding for about 8 years now with considerable experience in 8 languages and counting. Although the majority of my experience is in Java and Python, Swift has fascinated me ever since I took a class on it in my Sophomore year. I have since worked as a teaching assistant for that same class, allowing me to share my passion with others, as well as deepen my understanding of the language and its limitations.

My Personal Life

Outside of my main coursework, I keep busy. Throughout my time at IU I’ve continued expanding on my lifelong interest in the outdoors, mainly through IU’s Outdoor Adventures department. Over the years I’ve jumped from hobby to hobby, with some such as skateboarding going back as far as 8 years ago. In recent years I’ve taken up cycling and hiking as my go-to getaways, but I’m always looking for new ways to explore.
